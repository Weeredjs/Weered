## Full description (≤4000 chars)
```
Weered is a real-time community platform built for gaming communities and crews.

Lobbies you can actually live in. Live rooms with voice, presence, and chat. Bounties, crews, and per-game tools that surface what's happening right now — not last week.

WHAT'S INSIDE

• Live lobbies — drop into a Windrose, League, Fortnite, Destiny, PUBG, or Marathon lobby and see who's online, who's grouped up, who's streaming, what they're playing.

• Voice & text rooms — open or private rooms inside any lobby. Join one click, talk crossplay, drop in and out without scheduling a "session."

• Bounty board — post a paper bounty on anything (a sailor, a Kraken tooth, a rum run) and let hunters deliver proof. Stake's escrowed when you post — refunded only on cancel.

• Crews — long-term groups with a shared tag, roster, and presence. Your crew shows up wherever you go.

• Live presence — your friends list shows what game they're in, what platform, what they're streaming, in real time.

• Mod catalog — searchable Nexus-sourced mod listings for the games we cover.

• Ports of Call — community server registry merged with Steam's live server browser, so you find populated servers without alt-tabbing.

• Captain's Log — a weekly recap of everything that happened in your lobby, written by The Operator.

WHO IT'S FOR

If you remember MPlayer, Heat.net, Battle.net 1998 — Weered is what that should have grown into. Lobby-first. Place-first. Built for people who want to hang out in a community, not consume content.

Currently focused on:
• Windrose (PvE survival, just launched)
• League of Legends
• Fortnite
• Destiny 2
• PUBG
• Marathon

More games rolling in continuously.

NO SUBSCRIPTION REQUIRED

Free tier covers everything you need to use Weered with your crew. Paid tiers ($5–$14/mo) unlock cosmetic and convenience features, but nothing about the core lobby experience is gated.

PRIVACY

We don't sell data. We don't run ads. We don't track you off-platform. Read the full privacy policy at https://weered.ca/privacy.

FEEDBACK

We ship every day. If something's broken or missing, email support@weered.ca and you'll hear back same-day.
```
